Platform Engineering for Bahrain SaaS Companies

SaaS companies scaling from seed to Series C face the same DevOps inflection points. We've helped Bahrain SaaS teams - including EDB-backed startups - build the platform foundation that lets engineering scale without linearly increasing DevOps headcount.

Industry Challenges

What We See in This Space

Your SaaS platform serves customers across Bahrain, KSA, and the UAE - your deployment pipeline deploys to all regions simultaneously with no region-specific rollout control.
Your engineering team has grown from 5 to 25 engineers and your DevOps engineer is now a bottleneck for every deployment and environment request.
You need to implement SOC 2 Type II controls but your current infrastructure and deployment practices have no audit trail or access controls.

Bahrain SaaS companies hit the same DevOps ceiling: the informal ‘ask DevOps on Slack’ model breaks at 15-20 engineers, and the platform foundation needs to be rebuilt before it can scale. With the EDB actively supporting technology companies and Tamkeen providing workforce development grants, the growth pace of Bahrain’s SaaS sector means this inflection point arrives faster than founders expect.
