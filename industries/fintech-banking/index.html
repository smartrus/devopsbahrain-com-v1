DevOps for Bahrain's Regulated Financial Services

Fintech and banking DevOps isn't just about speed - it's about speed within regulatory constraints. We build delivery pipelines that satisfy CBB, CIBAFI, and card brand requirements without sacrificing deployment velocity.

What We See in This Space

Your CBB-licensed payment platform requires audit-logging of every production change - your current CI/CD has no change management trail.
Your Bahrain FinTech Bay-incubated application requires penetration testing evidence before each major release, but your pipeline has no security scanning integration.

Bahrain’s fintech sector - home to Bahrain FinTech Bay, the region’s leading fintech hub, and operating under CBB oversight - faces uniquely complex DevOps requirements. Deployment pipelines must maintain speed while satisfying regulators who care about change management, audit trails, and release approval processes.

Bahrain’s CBB regulatory sandbox programme (FinTech Regulatory Sandbox) has attracted fintech innovators from across the GCC and beyond. Whether you’re navigating the sandbox, scaling post-licence, or operating as an established digital bank, your DevOps practices must match the regulatory maturity your licence requires.

devopsbahrain.com works with CBB-licensed fintechs, digital banks, payment service providers, and Islamic finance institutions to build compliant-by-design DevOps pipelines - fast enough to compete, controlled enough to satisfy auditors.
