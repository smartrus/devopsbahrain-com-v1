Common Questions

DevOps Consulting Bahrain - Frequently Asked Questions

What DevOps services do you offer in Bahrain?

We offer the full DevOps spectrum for Bahrain engineering teams: platform engineering and internal developer platforms, CI/CD pipeline design and automation, cloud infrastructure on AWS me-south-1, Azure, or GCP, Site Reliability Engineering (SRE), AI-native DevOps and LLMOps, DevOps transformation assessments, and embedded DevOps staff augmentation. All services are delivered by senior engineers with GCC delivery experience.

How quickly can you embed a DevOps engineer in our Bahrain team?

We can have a senior DevOps engineer working with your team within one week. Our staff augmentation model embeds engineers directly into your team - your Slack, your standups, your codebase. This is a long-term team extension, not a short-term contractor rental.

Do you have experience with Bahrain's regulatory environment?

Yes. We work with Bahrain engineering teams navigating CBB (Central Bank of Bahrain) compliance requirements, PDPO data residency obligations, iGA digital government standards, and Tamkeen-supported digital transformation initiatives. Our CI/CD and infrastructure designs can incorporate compliance gates, audit logging, and data sovereignty controls from the start.

Which cloud regions do you recommend for Bahrain data residency?

AWS me-south-1 (Bahrain) is the primary region for data residency requirements under Bahraini law and CBB cloud guidance. We design infrastructure on AWS me-south-1 with multi-AZ resilience, and can architect hybrid or multi-cloud setups using Azure UAE North or GCP for workloads that don't require Bahrain-local residency. We advise on PDPO compliance and cloud policy alignment as part of every infrastructure engagement.

What is your approach to DevOps transformation for Bahrain companies?

We start with a 2–4 week DevOps maturity assessment: current pipeline audit, toolchain review, team skills evaluation, and delivery metrics baseline. We then deliver a prioritised 90-day transformation roadmap - with hands-on implementation, not a slide deck. We fix the blockers ourselves, working alongside your team, and leave you with documented systems your engineers can maintain.

Can you support EDB-backed startups and Bahrain FinTech Bay companies?

Yes. We regularly work with EDB-supported startups and Bahrain FinTech Bay companies. We understand the pace constraints of funded startups and the compliance requirements of CBB-regulated fintechs. Our platform engineering and CI/CD services are scoped for teams of 5–50 engineers and can be structured to align with EDB grant milestones.
